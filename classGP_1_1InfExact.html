Predict the mean and [co]variance.

Note
mu = ms + ks' * inv(Kn) * (y - m) = ms + ks' * alpha sigma^2 = kss + ks' * inv(Kn) * ks = kss + v' * v
Parameters
[in]logHypThe log hyperparameters.
[in]pXsThe test positions.
[out]pMuThe mean vector.
[out]pSigmaThe covariance matrix or variance vector.
[in]fVarianceVector(Optional) flag for true: variance vector, false: covariance matrix
[in]fBatchProcessing(Optional) flag for the batch processing.

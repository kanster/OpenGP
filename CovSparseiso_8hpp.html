class  GP::CovSparseiso< Scalar >
 Sparse covariance function with isotropic distances

\[ k(\mathbf{x}, \mathbf{z}) = \begin{cases} \sigma_f^2 \left( \frac{2+\cos(2\pi \frac{r}{l})}{3} \left(1-\frac{r}{l}\right) + \frac{1}{2\pi} \sin\left(2\pi \frac{r}{l}\right) \right) & r < l\\ 0 & r \ge l \end{cases} \]
